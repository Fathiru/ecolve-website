Reuse, Relove & Ecolve

Ecolve is an e-commerce platform designed for UNESA students to buy and sell preloved items in a simple, safe, and eco-friendly way. With a green-themed concept that represents sustainability, Ecolve encourages the culture of reuse, helps reduce waste, and provides students with access to quality items at affordable prices.
